Rediscover your outter beauty

Nic & Co is the starting point of your transformation. True beauty is the art that our team
passionately aims to create with our valued guests. Beautiful hairstyles begin with beautiful hair
and beautiful hair is healthy hair. Our core ambition is for you to look beautiful on the outside
while feeling truly beautiful on the inside.

Exclusive beauty
treatment

Offering the latest movements in style cuts, colours, Japanese straightening, and extensions, Nic & Co specialise in revealing the natural motion of the hair. Each individual service is tailored to the needs and style of each guest.Using sustainable organics or vegan brands that indulge botanical oils for healthy living, our products have been carefully selected to minimise the impact on your hair, body, and planet.


Personalized Care

Nic & Co is a small and intimate hair salon located on Brunswick Street in Brisbane’s New Farm. The space was created to empower beauty and relaxation with a genuine pamper experience for each valued guest. Highly experienced in all hair types.


We will find style that fits you!

With expert hairdressers ready to attend to your every hairdressing need,
you’ll always look and feel your best.
